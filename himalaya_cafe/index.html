Delicious Foods


Want the best Chai in town? Fruity for Mango Lassi? Hungry for yummy fried rice? The Himalaya Cafe offers these homely delights and more. Come and be warmly welcomed to enjoy Tibetan, Indian and Himalayian dishes served up with a smile.

Charity


The Himalaya Cafe occasionally runs charity events. Fund raising goes toward various worthy causes like helping children in India, Tibetan foundations and helping the disadvantaged living in the Himalayas. Come in to the cafe and ask how you can help brighten up somebody's day.

Fair Trade Shop


Rich, handwoven fabrics. Sturdy, warm, woolens. Colourful trinkets and decorations. Lots of goodies to choose from. Drop by the Himalaya Cafe to shop for your very own hand crafted Tibetan produce.
